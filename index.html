About Me

I currently work as a Tech Support Specialist for realties around the Portland area. When I'm not programming, I work on being the best father and husband I can be. I enjoy good craft beer, listening to vinyl records (oldies, jazz, and blues), photography, and sound technology.

You will see the handle, Mehequanna, associated with my work. It's a native american Algonquin name given to me by my father. Mehe means dancing/moving. Quanna means water. My father chose the name to show the correlation between my desire to be creative and friendliness (people like to hang out by water).
